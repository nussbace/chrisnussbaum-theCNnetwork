What



I am a self-taught coder and decided to create a personal website using programming languages HTML and CSS for the challenge of it and because I thought it would be fun to design something from scratch. I initially created this website to showcase my capstone project to my professors, classmates, friends, and family of Pacific Lutheran University due to the COVID-19 pandemic but realized I could use this website to network for the long-term. On this website, there is an About tab, a Capstone tab, and a Portfolio tab which contain their respective content. Have a look, and check out my social media while you're at it!




Mission



The objective of The CN Network is to network with employers from all over the web within the job market. What I want to gain from this website is to create connections with employers, college-students, and anyone wanting to illustrate their accomplishments and believe they have more to offer of themselves than their resume. A future goal I hope to achieve with this network is to pave an avenue for college students who are in search of jobs post-graduation. This platform can be used to showcase their identity in many forms (blogs, projects, resume, social media, etc.), and will give employers multiple angles and perspectives of their character.
